QAKE THE SNAKE

A fast, chaotic city-rampage action game where your snake grows from every kill. Smash through streets, trigger absurd bonuses, survive police escalation, and light up the entire skyline. Available on desktop and Android. Built with Golang and OpenGL.

Download Now Watch Screenshots View Source

Qake The Snake blends tight movement, large-scale destruction, and outrageous powerups into short sessions that stay intense the whole run.

Urban Destruction

Tear through building blocks, crack roads, and trigger chain explosions that reshape the map.

Swarm Chaos

Split into ghost segments, hunt targets, and reunite into one massive predator.

Escalating Heat

Cops and military pressure intensify as your run grows deadlier.

Wild Bonuses

Missiles, gatling, vacuum, surge, firestorms, tactical nukes, exploding pigs, air support and more many more...
